How to share with our artist friends

Send an email directly to the submission email address.

Put the title of your post in the Subject line of the email.

Note: Photo posts ignore the subject line, so if you want to talk about your photos put something in the body of the email.

Put the work you want to share in the body of the email.

  • Write text - format it however you want.
  • Attach photos - as many as you can handle (almost - maximum of 10 per post)
  • To share music - put a Dropbox link or a Soundcloud embed in the body of the email.

Add your artist/author name as a tag anywhere in the email body

like so: #pecospryor

Please also add additional tags like #music #poetry #text #mycoolprojectwithmultipleposts #photography - these are searchable/sortable later on.

F A Q

How do I send a Text post?

  1. Put the text in the body of the email.
  2. Put a title in the subject line (optional).

How do I send a Photo or many photos?

  1. Attach photos to the email.
  2. Write a caption in the subject line or body.

How do I send music?

  1. Upload your work to Soundcloud or Dropbox
  2. Put the SoundCloud embed link or Dropbox link into the body of the email.

Can I use Markdown to create rich text formatting when posting by email?

  1. Why yes, you can: just be sure to include !m in any part of the email’s subject or body to enable Markdown. The !m code will automatically be removed from the final version.

Where is the website?

  1. http://www.xnlvg.com and then click on the nice colors, then enter your password.

Why email? Why not some submission portal?

  1. Because text. Because the internet. Just add the email address to your contact book, and you never have to point and click, you can share from anywhere, even with a bad connection. You could even share from Fjaðrárgljúfur.

A few more tips: 

  • Send posts directly to your mobile publishing email address. You cannot email another email address and then forward the email from there.
  • Make sure to put the publishing email address in the “To:” field; it cannot go in the “cc:” or “bcc:” field.
  • Make sure to add tags! Otherwise nobody will ever know who did it!
