How to share with our artist friends

Navigate to the submission page, or login to your Tumblr invitation.

Navigate to the /submit page, or log in to your Tumblr account.

Choose your content type or click on one of the friendly post type options in the Tumblr portal.

*To share music - use the Tumblr login portal, or use the "Link" option on the Submit page.

Add/choose your tags including your author name.

like so: #vincentvangogh

Please also add additional tags like #music #poetry #text #photography - these are searchable/sortable later on.

F A Q

Where is the website?

  1. http://www.xnlvg.com and then click on the nice colors, then enter your password.

How do I edit my post?

  1. Login to the Tumblr portal via the email invitation link you received. If you need a new invitation link, click here and enter our password.

How do I send a Photo or many photos?

  1. Use the 'Photo' dropdown on the Submit page, or login to the Tumblr portal.

How do I send music?

  1. Upload your work to Soundcloud or Dropbox
  2. Put the SoundCloud embed link or Dropbox link into the body of the email.

A few more tips:

  • For new stanzas in written work, add a double line break.
  • To seperate written work from your commentary (if you desire to comment), add the HTML snippet <hr> and you'll get a stylish little divider line, like the one you see at the bottom of this FAQ.
  • Make sure to add tags! Otherwise nobody will ever know who did it!
